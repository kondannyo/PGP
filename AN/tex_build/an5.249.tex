{\PaliGlossB{249. A Charnel Ground}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā sivathikāya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Mendicants, there are these five drawbacks to a charnel ground.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katame pañca?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What five?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s filthy, stinking, frightening, a gathering place for savage monsters, and a weeping place for many people.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.5}

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcime ādīnavā sivathikūpame puggale.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way there are five drawbacks of a person like a charnel ground.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{To start with, some person has filthy conduct by way of body, speech, and mind.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.7}

{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idamassa asucitāya vadāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is how they’re filthy, I say.}}\\
\end{addmargin}

{\PaliGlossB{That person is just as filthy as a charnel ground.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Because of their filthy conduct, they get a bad reputation.}}\\

\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idamassa duggandhatāya vadāmi.}}\\

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
