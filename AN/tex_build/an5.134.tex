{\PaliGlossA{14. rājavagga}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{14. Kings}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{134. yassaṃdisaṃsutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{134. In Whatever Region}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato rājā khattiyo muddhāvasitto yassaṃ yassaṃ disāyaṃ viharati, sakasmiṃyeva vijite viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Mendicants, with five factors an anointed aristocratic king lives in his own realm, no matter what region he lives in.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katamehi pañcahi?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What five?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha, bhikkhave, rājā khattiyo muddhāvasitto ubhato sujāto hoti mātito ca pitito ca, saṃsuddhagahaṇiko, yāva sattamā pitāmahayugā akkhitto anupakkuṭṭho jātivādena;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{An anointed aristocratic king is well born on both his mother’s and father’s side, of pure descent, irrefutable and impeccable in questions of ancestry back to the seventh paternal generation.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{aḍḍho hoti mahaddhano mahābhogo paripuṇṇakosakoṭṭhāgāro;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He is rich, affluent, and wealthy, with a full treasury and storehouses.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{balavā kho pana hoti caturaṅginiyā senāya samannāgato assavāya ovādapaṭikarāya;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He is powerful, having an army of four divisions that is obedient and carries out instructions.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{pariṇāyako kho panassa hoti paṇḍito viyatto medhāvī paṭibalo atītānāgatapaccuppanne atthe cintetuṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He has a counselor who is astute, competent, and intelligent, able to think issues through as they bear upon the past, future, and present.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassime cattāro dhammā yasaṃ paripācenti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{These four things bring his fame to fruition.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so iminā yasapañcamena dhammena samannāgato yassaṃ yassaṃ disāyaṃ viharati, sakasmiṃyeva vijite viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{With these five factors, including fame, an anointed aristocratic king lives in his own realm, no matter what direction he lives in.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{taṃ kissa hetu?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Why is that?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evañhetaṃ, bhikkhave, hoti vijitāvīnaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Because that is how it is for victors.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi dhammehi samannāgato bhikkhu yassaṃ yassaṃ disāyaṃ viharati, vimuttacittova viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, a mendicant with five qualities lives with mind freed, no matter what region they live in.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katamehi pañcahi?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What five?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sīlavā hoti, pātimokkhasaṃvarasaṃvuto viharati ācāragocarasampanno aṇumattesu vajjesu bhayadassāvī, samādāya sikkhati sikkhāpadesu—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s when mendicant is ethical, restrained in the monastic code, conducting themselves well and seeking alms in suitable places. Seeing danger in the slightest fault, they keep the rules they’ve undertaken.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{rājāva khattiyo muddhāvasitto jātisampanno;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is like the anointed aristocratic king’s impeccable lineage.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bahussuto hoti sutadharo sutasannicayo, ye te dhammā ādikalyāṇā majjhekalyāṇā pariyosānakalyāṇā sātthaṃ sabyañjanaṃ kevalaparipuṇṇaṃ parisuddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ abhivadanti, tathārūpāssa dhammā bahussutā honti dhātā vacasā paricitā manasānupekkhitā diṭṭhiyā suppaṭividdhā—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They’re very learned, remembering and keeping what they’ve learned. These teachings are good in the beginning, good in the middle, and good in the end, meaningful and well-phrased, describing a spiritual practice that’s entirely full and pure. They are very learned in such teachings, remembering them, reinforcing them by recitation, mentally scrutinizing them, and comprehending them theoretically.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{rājāva khattiyo muddhāvasitto aḍḍho mahaddhano mahābhogo paripuṇṇakosakoṭṭhāgāro;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is like the anointed aristocratic king being rich, affluent, and wealthy, with full treasury and storehouses.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{āraddhavīriyo viharati akusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ pahānāya kusalānaṃ dhammānaṃ upasampadāya thāmavā daḷhaparakkamo anikkhittadhuro kusalesu dhammesu—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They live with energy roused up for giving up unskillful qualities and embracing skillful qualities. They’re strong, staunchly vigorous, not slacking off when it comes to developing skillful qualities.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{rājāva khattiyo muddhāvasitto balasampanno;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is like the anointed aristocratic king having power.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{paññavā hoti udayatthagāminiyā paññāya samannāgato ariyāya nibbedhikāya sammā dukkhakkhayagāminiyā—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They’re wise. They have the wisdom of arising and passing away which is noble, penetrative, and leads to the complete ending of suffering.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{rājāva khattiyo muddhāvasitto pariṇāyakasampanno;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is like the anointed aristocratic king having a counselor.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassime cattāro dhammā vimuttiṃ paripācenti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{These four qualities bring their freedom to fruition.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so iminā vimuttipañcamena dhammena samannāgato yassaṃ yassaṃ disāyaṃ viharati vimuttacittova viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{With these five qualities, including freedom, they live in their own realm, no matter what region they live in.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{taṃ kissa hetu?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Why is that?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evañhetaṃ, bhikkhave, hoti vimuttacittānan”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Because that is how it is for those whose mind is free.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
