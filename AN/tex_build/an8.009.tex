{\PaliGlossB{and ‘lustful’.}}\\
\end{addmargin}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{kimaññatra, bhikkhave, nando indriyesu guttadvāro, bhojane mattaññū, jāgariyaṃ anuyutto, satisampajaññena samannāgato, yehi nando sakkoti paripuṇṇaṃ parisuddhaṃ brahmacariyaṃ carituṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{How could he live the full and pure spiritual life unless he guards the sense doors, eats in moderation, is dedicated to wakefulness, and has mindfulness and situational awareness?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is how Nanda guards the sense doors.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{If he has to look to the east, he wholeheartedly concentrates before looking, thinking:}}\\

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘When I look to the east, bad, unskillful qualities of desire and aversion will not overwhelm me.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
