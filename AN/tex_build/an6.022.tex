{\PaliGlossA{22. aparihāniyasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{22. Non-decline}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“chayime, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi, taṃ suṇātha … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Mendicants, I will teach you these six qualities that prevent decline. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katame ca, bhikkhave, cha aparihāniyā dhammā?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And what, mendicants, are the six qualities that prevent decline?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{na kammārāmatā, na bhassārāmatā, na niddārāmatā, na saṅgaṇikārāmatā, sovacassatā, kalyāṇamittatā—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Not relishing work, talk, sleep, and company, being easy to admonish, and having good friends.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ime kho, bhikkhave, cha aparihāniyā dhammā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{These six qualities prevent decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ye hi keci, bhikkhave, atītamaddhānaṃ na parihāyiṃsu kusalehi dhammehi, sabbete imeheva chahi dhammehi na parihāyiṃsu kusalehi dhammehi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Whether in the past, future, or present, all those who have not declined in skillful qualities do so because of these six qualities.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
