\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho āyasmā saddho yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ saddhaṃ bhagavā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“ājānīyajhāyitaṃ kho, saddha, jhāya;}}\\

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{not like a wild colt.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{kathañca, khaḷuṅkajhāyitaṃ hoti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And how does a wild colt meditate?}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A wild colt, tied up by the feeding trough, meditates: ‘Fodder, fodder!’}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

{\PaliGlossB{Why is that?}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Because it doesn’t occur to the wild colt tied up by the feeding trough:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

{\PaliGlossA{so doṇiyā baddho ‘yavasaṃ yavasan’ti jhāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Tied up by the feeding trough they just meditate: ‘Fodder, fodder!’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, saddha, idhekacco purisakhaḷuṅko araññagatopi rukkhamūlagatopi suññāgāragatopi kāmarāgapariyuṭṭhitena cetasā viharati kāmarāgaparetena uppannassa ca kāmarāgassa nissaraṇaṃ yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, take a certain wild person who has gone to the forest, the root of a tree, or an empty hut. Their heart is overcome and mired in sensual desire, and they don’t truly understand the escape from sensual desire that has arisen.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Harboring sensual desire within they meditate and concentrate and contemplate and ruminate.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.7}

\setstretch{.5}

They meditate dependent on what is seen, heard, thought, known, attained, sought, or explored by the mind.}}\\
