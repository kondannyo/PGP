{\PaliGlossA{182. pāṭibhogasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{182. Guarantee}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“catunnaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ natthi koci pāṭibhogo—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“There are four things that no-one can guarantee—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{samaṇo vā brāhmaṇo vā devo vā māro vā brahmā vā koci vā lokasmiṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{not an ascetic, a brahmin, a god, a Māra, a Brahmā, or anyone in the world.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katamesaṃ catunnaṃ?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What four?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘jarādhammaṃ mā jīrī’ti natthi koci pāṭibhogo—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{No-one can guarantee that someone liable to grow old will not grow old.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{samaṇo vā brāhmaṇo vā devo vā māro vā brahmā vā koci vā lokasmiṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘byādhidhammaṃ mā byādhiyī’ti natthi koci pāṭibhogo—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{No-one can guarantee that someone liable to sickness will not get sick.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{samaṇo vā brāhmaṇo vā devo vā māro vā brahmā vā koci vā lokasmiṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘maraṇadhammaṃ mā mīyī’ti natthi koci pāṭibhogo—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{No-one can guarantee that someone liable to death will not die.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{samaṇo vā brāhmaṇo vā devo vā māro vā brahmā vā koci vā lokasmiṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘yāni kho pana tāni pubbe attanā katāni pāpakāni kammāni saṅkilesikāni ponobhavikāni sadarāni dukkhavipākāni āyatiṃ jātijarāmaraṇikāni, tesaṃ vipāko mā nibbattī’ti natthi koci pāṭibhogo—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{No-one can guarantee that the bad deeds done in past lives—corrupted, leading to future lives, hurtful, resulting in suffering and future rebirth, old age, and death—will not produce their result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{samaṇo vā brāhmaṇo vā devo vā māro vā brahmā vā koci vā lokasmiṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{These are the four things that no-one can guarantee—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{not an ascetic, a brahmin, a god, a Māra, a Brahmā, or anyone in the world.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
