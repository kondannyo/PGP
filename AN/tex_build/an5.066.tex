{\PaliGlossB{A mendicant is personally accomplished in ethics, and answers questions posed when discussing accomplishment in ethics.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They’re personally accomplished in wisdom, and they answer questions posed when discussing accomplishment in wisdom.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
