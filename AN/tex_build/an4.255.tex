{\PaliGlossA{idha, bhikkhave, ekacco attanā jarādhammo samāno jarādhamme ādīnavaṃ viditvā ajaraṃ anuttaraṃ yogakkhemaṃ nibbānaṃ pariyesati;}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone who is liable to get sick, knowing the drawback in what gets sick, searches for the sickness-free supreme sanctuary, extinguishment.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
