{\PaliGlossA{57. chaḷabhijātisutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{57. The Six Classes of Rebirth}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati gijjhakūṭe pabbate.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Rājagaha, on the Vulture’s Peak Mountain.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho āyasmā ānando yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā ānando bhagavantaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then Venerable Ānanda went up to the Buddha, bowed, sat down to one side, and said to him:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“pūraṇena, bhante, kassapena chaḷabhijātiyo paññattā—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Sir, Pūraṇa Kassapa describes six classes of rebirth:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{kaṇhābhijāti paññattā, nīlābhijāti paññattā, lohitābhijāti paññattā, haliddābhijāti paññattā, sukkābhijāti paññattā, paramasukkābhijāti paññattā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{black, blue, red, yellow, white, and ultimate white.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena kaṇhābhijāti paññattā, orabbhikā sūkarikā sākuṇikā māgavikā luddā macchaghātakā corā coraghātakā bandhanāgārikā ye vā panaññepi keci kurūrakammantā. (1)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The black class of rebirth consists of slaughterers of sheep, pigs, poultry, or deer, hunters or fishers, bandits, executioners, butchers of cattle, jailers, and any others with a cruel livelihood.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena nīlābhijāti paññattā, bhikkhū kaṇṭakavuttikā ye vā panaññepi keci kammavādā kriyavādā. (2)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The blue class of rebirth consists of mendicants who live on thorns, and any others who teach the efficacy of deeds and action.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena lohitābhijāti paññattā, nigaṇṭhā ekasāṭakā. (3)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The red class of rebirth consists of the Jain ascetics who wear one cloth.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena haliddābhijāti paññattā, gihī odātavasanā acelakasāvakā. (4)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The yellow class of rebirth consists of the lay people dressed in white who are disciples of the naked ascetics.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena sukkābhijāti paññattā, ājīvakā ājīvakiniyo. (5)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The white class of rebirth consists of male and female <i>Ājīvaka</i> ascetics.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatridaṃ, bhante, pūraṇena kassapena paramasukkābhijāti paññattā, nando vaccho kiso saṅkicco makkhali gosālo. (6)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And the ultimate white class of rebirth consists of Nanda Vaccha, Kisa Saṅkicca, and Makkhali Gosāla.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{pūraṇena, bhante, kassapena imā chaḷabhijātiyo paññattā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{These are the six classes of rebirth that Pūraṇa Kassapa describes.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“kiṃ panānanda, pūraṇassa kassapassa sabbo loko etadabbhanujānāti imā chaḷabhijātiyo paññāpetun”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“But Ānanda, did the whole world authorize Pūraṇa Kassapa to describe these six classes of rebirth?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“no hetaṃ, bhante.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“No, sir.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, ānanda, puriso daliddo assako anāḷhiko, tassa akāmakassa bilaṃ olaggeyyuṃ: ‘idaṃ te, ambho purisa, maṃsañca khāditabbaṃ, mūlañca anuppadātabban’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“It’s as if they were to force a steak on a poor, penniless person, telling them they must eat it and then pay for it.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, ānanda, pūraṇena kassapena appaṭiññāya etesaṃ samaṇabrāhmaṇānaṃ imā chaḷabhijātiyo paññattā, yathā taṃ bālena abyattena akhettaññunā akusalena.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, Pūraṇa Kassapa has described these six classes of rebirth without the consent of those ascetics and brahmins. And he has done so in a foolish, incompetent, unskilled way, lacking common sense.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ahaṃ kho panānanda, chaḷabhijātiyo paññāpemi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I, however, also describe six classes of rebirth.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{taṃ suṇāhi, sādhukaṃ manasi karohi; bhāsissāmī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Listen and pay close attention, I will speak.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“evaṃ, bhante”ti kho āyasmā ānando bhagavato paccassosi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Yes, sir,” Ānanda replied.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bhagavā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The Buddha said this:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“katamā cānanda, chaḷabhijātiyo?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“And what, Ānanda, are the six classes of rebirth?}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idhānanda, ekacco kaṇhābhijātiyo samāno kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a dark class gives rise to a dark result.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha panānanda, ekacco kaṇhābhijātiyo samāno sukkaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a dark class gives rise to a bright result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha panānanda, ekacco kaṇhābhijātiyo samāno akaṇhaṃ asukkaṃ nibbānaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a dark class gives rise to extinguishment, which is neither dark nor bright.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha panānanda, ekacco sukkābhijātiyo samāno kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a bright class gives rise to a dark result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha panānanda, ekacco sukkābhijātiyo samāno sukkaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a bright class gives rise to a bright result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idha panānanda, ekacco sukkābhijātiyo samāno akaṇhaṃ asukkaṃ nibbānaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Someone born into a bright class gives rise to extinguishment, which is neither dark nor bright.}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{kathañcānanda, kaṇhābhijātiyo samāno kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And how does someone born into a dark class give rise to a dark result?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idhānanda, ekacco nīce kule paccājāto hoti—caṇḍālakule vā nesādakule vā venakule vā rathakārakule vā pukkusakule vā, dalidde appannapānabhojane kasiravuttike, yattha kasirena ghāsacchādo labbhati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s when someone is reborn in a low family—a family of outcastes, bamboo-workers, hunters, chariot-makers, or waste-collectors—poor, with little to eat or drink, where life is tough, and food and shelter are hard to find.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so ca hoti dubbaṇṇo duddasiko okoṭimako bavhābādho kāṇo vā kuṇī vā khañjo vā pakkhahato vā, na lābhī annassa pānassa vatthassa yānassa mālāgandhavilepanassa seyyāvasathapadīpeyyassa.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And they’re ugly, unsightly, deformed, chronically ill—one-eyed, crippled, lame, or half-paralyzed. They don’t get to have food, drink, clothes, and vehicles; garlands, fragrance, and makeup; or bed, house, and lighting.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so kāyena duccaritaṃ carati, vācāya duccaritaṃ carati, manasā duccaritaṃ carati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And they do bad things by way of body, speech, and mind.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When their body breaks up, after death, they’re reborn in a place of loss, a bad place, the underworld, hell.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evaṃ kho, ānanda, kaṇhābhijātiyo samāno kaṇhaṃ dhammaṃ abhijāyati. (1)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That’s how someone born into a dark class gives rise to a dark result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And how does someone born into a dark class give rise to a bright result?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idhānanda, ekacco nīce kule paccājāto hoti—caṇḍālakule vā … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s when some person is reborn in a low family …}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.5}

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s when some person is reborn in an eminent family …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

{\PaliGlossA{seyyāvasathapadīpeyyassa.}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And they do good things by way of body, speech, and mind.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so kāyena sucaritaṃ caritvā, vācāya sucaritaṃ caritvā, manasā sucaritaṃ caritvā kāyassa bhedā paraṃ maraṇā sugatiṃ saggaṃ lokaṃ upapajjati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When their body breaks up, after death, they’re reborn in a good place, a heavenly realm.}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That’s how someone born into a bright class give rise to a bright result.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And how does someone born into a bright class give rise to extinguishment, which is neither dark nor bright?}}\\

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It’s when some person is reborn in an eminent family …}}\\

\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so ca hoti abhirūpo dassanīyo pāsādiko paramāya vaṇṇapokkharatāya samannāgato, lābhī annassa pānassa vatthassa yānassa mālāgandhavilepanassa seyyāvasathapadīpeyyassa.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They shave off their hair and beard, dress in ocher robes, and go forth from the lay life to homelessness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so evaṃ pabbajito samāno pañca nīvaraṇe pahāya cetaso upakkilese paññāya dubbalīkaraṇe, catūsu satipaṭṭhānesu suppatiṭṭhitacitto, satta bojjhaṅge yathābhūtaṃ bhāvetvā akaṇhaṃ asukkaṃ nibbānaṃ abhijāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They give up the five hindrances, corruptions of the heart that weaken wisdom. They firmly establish their mind in the four kinds of mindfulness meditation. They truly develop the seven awakening factors. And then they give rise to extinguishment, which is neither dark nor bright.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That’s how someone born into a bright class gives rise to extinguishment, which is neither dark nor bright.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
