{\PaliGlossA{194. kāraṇapālīsutta}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{194. With Kāraṇapālī}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā vesāliyaṃ viharati mahāvane kūṭāgārasālāyaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Vesālī, at the Great Wood, in the hall with the peaked roof.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tena kho pana samayena kāraṇapālī brāhmaṇo licchavīnaṃ kammantaṃ kāreti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Now at that time the brahmin Kāraṇapālī was working for the Licchavis.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{addasā kho kāraṇapālī brāhmaṇo piṅgiyāniṃ brāhmaṇaṃ dūratova āgacchantaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He saw the brahmin Piṅgiyānī coming off in the distance}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{disvā piṅgiyāniṃ brāhmaṇaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{and said to him,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“handa kuto nu bhavaṃ piṅgiyānī āgacchati divā divassā”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“So, Piṅgiyānī, where are you coming from in the middle of the day?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“itohaṃ, bho, āgacchāmi samaṇassa gotamassa santikā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“I’m coming, my good man, from the presence of the ascetic Gotama.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“taṃ kiṃ maññati bhavaṃ piṅgiyānī samaṇassa gotamassa paññāveyyattiyaṃ? paṇḍito maññe”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“What do you think of the ascetic Gotama’s lucidity of wisdom? Do you think he’s astute?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“ko cāhaṃ, bho, ko ca samaṇassa gotamassa paññāveyyattiyaṃ jānissāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“My good man, who am I to judge the ascetic Gotama’s lucidity of wisdom?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{sopi nūnassa tādisova yo samaṇassa gotamassa paññāveyyattiyaṃ jāneyyā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{You’d really have to be on the same level to judge his lucidity of wisdom.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“uḷārāya khalu bhavaṃ piṅgiyānī samaṇaṃ gotamaṃ pasaṃsāya pasaṃsatī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Master Piṅgiyānī praises the ascetic Gotama with magnificent praise indeed.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“ko cāhaṃ, bho, ko ca samaṇaṃ gotamaṃ pasaṃsissāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Who am I to praise the ascetic Gotama?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{pasatthappasatthova so bhavaṃ gotamo seṭṭho devamanussānan”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He is praised by the praised as the best of gods and humans.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“kiṃ pana bhavaṃ piṅgiyānī atthavasaṃ sampassamāno samaṇe gotame evaṃ abhippasanno”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“But for what reason are you so devoted to the ascetic Gotama?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“seyyathāpi, bho, puriso aggarasaparititto na aññesaṃ hīnānaṃ rasānaṃ piheti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Suppose a person was completely satisfied by the best tasting food. They wouldn’t be attracted to anything that tasted inferior.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bho, yato yato tassa bhoto gotamassa dhammaṃ suṇāti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, when you hear the ascetic Gotama’s teaching—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi suttaso, yadi geyyaso, yadi veyyākaraṇaso, yadi abbhutadhammaso—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whatever it may be, whether statements, songs, discussions, or amazing stories—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tato tato na aññesaṃ puthusamaṇabrāhmaṇappavādānaṃ piheti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{then you’re not attracted to the doctrines of the various ascetics and brahmins.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bho, puriso jighacchādubbalyapareto madhupiṇḍikaṃ adhigaccheyya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose a person who was weak with hunger was to obtain a honey-cake.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so yato yato sāyetha, labhateva sādurasaṃ asecanakaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Wherever they taste it, they would enjoy a sweet, delicious flavor.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bho, yato yato tassa bhoto gotamassa dhammaṃ suṇāti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, when you hear the ascetic Gotama’s teaching—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi suttaso, yadi geyyaso, yadi veyyākaraṇaso, yadi abbhutadhammaso—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whatever it may be, whether statements, songs, discussions, or amazing stories—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tato tato labhateva attamanataṃ, labhati cetaso pasādaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{then you get a sense of uplift, a confidence of the heart.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bho, puriso candanaghaṭikaṃ adhigaccheyya—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose a person were to obtain a piece of sandalwood,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{haricandanassa vā lohitacandanassa vā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whether yellow or red.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so yato yato ghāyetha—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Wherever they smelled it—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi mūlato, yadi majjhato, yadi aggato—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whether at the root, the middle, or the top—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{adhigacchateva surabhigandhaṃ asecanakaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they’d enjoy a delicious fragrance.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bho, yato yato tassa bhoto gotamassa dhammaṃ suṇāti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, when you hear the ascetic Gotama’s teaching—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi suttaso, yadi geyyaso, yadi veyyākaraṇaso, yadi abbhutadhammaso—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whatever it may be, whether statements, songs, discussions, or amazing stories—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tato tato adhigacchati pāmojjaṃ adhigacchati somanassaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{then you become filled with joy and happiness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bho, puriso ābādhiko dukkhito bāḷhagilāno.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose there was a person who was sick, suffering, gravely ill.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa kusalo bhisakko ṭhānaso ābādhaṃ nīhareyya;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A good doctor would cure them on the spot.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bho, yato yato tassa bhoto gotamassa dhammaṃ suṇāti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, when you hear the ascetic Gotama’s teaching—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi suttaso, yadi geyyaso, yadi veyyākaraṇaso, yadi abbhutadhammaso—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whatever it may be, whether statements, songs, discussions, or amazing stories—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tato tato sokaparidevadukkhadomanassupāyāsā abbhatthaṃ gacchanti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{then you make an end of sorrow, lamentation, pain, sadness, and distress.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bho, pokkharaṇī acchodakā sātodakā sītodakā setakā supatitthā ramaṇīyā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose there was a lotus pond with clear, sweet, cool water, clean, with smooth banks, delightful.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha puriso āgaccheyya ghammābhitatto ghammapareto kilanto tasito pipāsito.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then along comes a person struggling in the oppressive heat, weary, thirsty, and parched.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so taṃ pokkharaṇiṃ ogāhetvā nhātvā ca pivitvā ca sabbadarathakilamathapariḷāhaṃ paṭippassambheyya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They’d plunge into the lotus pond to bathe and drink. And all their stress, weariness, and heat exhaustion would die down.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evamevaṃ kho, bho, yato yato tassa bhoto gotamassa dhammaṃ suṇāti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, when you hear the ascetic Gotama’s teaching—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadi suttaso, yadi geyyaso, yadi veyyākaraṇaso, yadi abbhutadhammaso—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{whatever it may be, whether statements, songs, discussions, or amazing stories—}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tato tato sabbadarathakilamathapariḷāhā paṭippassambhantī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{then all your stress, weariness, and exhaustion die down.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evaṃ vutte, kāraṇapālī brāhmaṇo uṭṭhāyāsanā ekaṃsaṃ uttarāsaṅgaṃ karitvā dakkhiṇaṃ jāṇumaṇḍalaṃ pathaviyaṃ nihantvā yena bhagavā tenañjaliṃ paṇāmetvā tikkhattuṃ udānaṃ udānesi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When this was said, the brahmin Kāraṇapālī got up from his seat, arranged his robe over one shoulder, knelt on his right knee, raised his joined palms toward the Buddha, and was inspired to exclaim three times:}}\\
