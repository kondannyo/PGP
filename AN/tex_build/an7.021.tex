{\PaliGlossA{3. vajjisattakavagga}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{3. The Vajji Seven}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{21. sārandadasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{21. At Sārandada}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evaṃ me sutaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{So I have heard.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā vesāliyaṃ viharati sārandade cetiye.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Vesālī, at the Sārandada Tree-shrine.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho sambahulā licchavī yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkamiṃsu; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdiṃsu. ekamantaṃ nisinne kho te licchavī bhagavā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then several Licchavis went up to the Buddha, bowed, sat down to one side, and the Buddha said to these Licchavis:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“satta vo, licchavī, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Licchavis, I will teach you these seven principles that prevent decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{taṃ suṇātha, sādhukaṃ manasi karotha, bhāsissāmī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Listen and pay close attention, I will speak.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“evaṃ, bhante”ti kho te licchavī bhagavato paccassosuṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Yes, sir,” they replied.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bhagavā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The Buddha said this:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“katame ca, licchavī, satta aparihāniyā dhammā?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“And what are the seven principles that prevent decline?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī abhiṇhaṃ sannipātā bhavissanti sannipātabahulā;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis meet frequently and have many meetings,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (1)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī samaggā sannipatissanti, samaggā vuṭṭhahissanti, samaggā vajjikaraṇīyāni karissanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis meet in harmony, leave in harmony, and carry on their business in harmony,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (2)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī apaññattaṃ na paññāpessanti, paññattaṃ na samucchindissanti, yathāpaññatte porāṇe vajjidhamme samādāya vattissanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis don’t make new decrees or abolish existing decrees, but undertake and follow the traditional Vajjian principles as they have been decreed,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (3)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī ye te vajjīnaṃ vajjimahallakā te sakkarissanti garuṃ karissanti mānessanti pūjessanti, tesañca sotabbaṃ maññissanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis honor, respect, esteem, and venerate Vajjian elders, and think them worth listening to,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (4)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī yā tā kulitthiyo kulakumāriyo tā na okassa pasayha vāsessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis don’t rape or abduct women or girls from their families and force them to live with them,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (5)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjī yāni tāni vajjīnaṃ vajjicetiyāni abbhantarāni ceva bāhirāni ca tāni sakkarissanti garuṃ karissanti mānessanti pūjessanti, tesañca dinnapubbaṃ katapubbaṃ dhammikaṃ baliṃ no parihāpessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis honor, respect, esteem, and venerate the Vajjian shrines, whether inner or outer, not neglecting the proper spirit-offerings that were given and made in the past,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (6)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ arahantesu dhammikā rakkhāvaraṇagutti susaṃvihitā bhavissati:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as the Vajjis arrange for proper protection, shelter, and security for perfected ones, so that}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘kinti anāgatā ca arahanto vijitaṃ āgaccheyyuṃ, āgatā ca arahanto vijite phāsuṃ vihareyyun’ti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{more perfected ones might come to the realm and those already here may live in comfort,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihāni. (7)}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yāvakīvañca, licchavī, ime satta aparihāniyā dhammā vajjīsu ṭhassanti, imesu ca sattasu aparihāniyesu dhammesu vajjī sandississanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{As long as these seven principles that prevent decline last among the Vajjis, and as long as the Vajjis are seen following them,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vuddhiyeva, licchavī, vajjīnaṃ pāṭikaṅkhā, no parihānī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{they can expect growth, not decline.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
