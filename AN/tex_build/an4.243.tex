{\PaliGlossA{25. āpattibhayavagga}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{243. saṅghabhedakasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{243. Schism in the Saṅgha}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā kosambiyaṃ viharati ghositārāme.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Kosambi, in Ghosita’s Monastery.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho āyasmā ānando yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ ānandaṃ bhagavā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then Venerable Ānanda went up to the Buddha, bowed, and sat down to one side. The Buddha said to him,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“api nu taṃ, ānanda, adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasantan”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Well, Ānanda, has that disciplinary issue been settled yet?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“kuto taṃ, bhante, adhikaraṇaṃ vūpasamissati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“How could it be, sir?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{āyasmato, bhante, anuruddhassa bāhiyo nāma saddhivihāriko kevalakappaṃ saṃghabhedāya ṭhito.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Venerable Anuruddha’s pupil Bāhiya remains entirely committed to creating a schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tatrāyasmā anuruddho na ekavācikampi bhaṇitabbaṃ maññatī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{But Anuruddha doesn’t think to say a single word about it.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“kadā panānanda, anuruddho saṃghamajjhe adhikaraṇesu voyuñjati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“But Ānanda, since when has Anuruddha been involved in disciplinary issues in the midst of the Saṅgha?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{nanu, ānanda, yāni kānici adhikaraṇāni uppajjanti, sabbāni tāni tumhe ceva vūpasametha sāriputtamoggallānā ca.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Shouldn’t you, together with Sāriputta and Moggallāna, settle all disciplinary issues that come up?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{cattārome, ānanda, atthavase sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṅghabhedena nandati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees four reasons to relish schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katame cattāro?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What four?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idhānanda, pāpabhikkhu dussīlo hoti pāpadhammo asuci saṅkassarasamācāro paṭicchannakammanto assamaṇo samaṇapaṭiñño abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño antopūti avassuto kasambujāto.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Take an unethical monk, of bad qualities, filthy, with suspicious behavior, underhand, no true ascetic or spiritual practitioner—though claiming to be one—rotten inside, corrupt, and depraved.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa evaṃ hoti:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He thinks:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘sace kho maṃ bhikkhū jānissanti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Suppose the monks know that I’m a bad monk …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{dussīlo pāpadhammo asuci saṅkassarasamācāro paṭicchannakammanto assamaṇo samaṇapaṭiñño abrahmacārī brahmacāripaṭiñño antopūti avassuto kasambujātoti, samaggā maṃ santā nāsessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{If they’re in harmony, they’ll expel me,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vaggā pana maṃ na nāsessantī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{but if they’re divided they won’t.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idaṃ, ānanda, paṭhamaṃ atthavasaṃ sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṅghabhedena nandati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees this as the first reason to relish schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{puna caparaṃ, ānanda, pāpabhikkhu micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, antaggāhikāya diṭṭhiyā samannāgato.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Furthermore, a bad monk has wrong view, he’s attached to an extremist view.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa evaṃ hoti:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He thinks:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘sace kho maṃ bhikkhū jānissanti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Suppose the monks know that I have wrong view …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{micchādiṭṭhiko antaggāhikāya diṭṭhiyā samannāgatoti, samaggā maṃ santā nāsessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{If they’re in harmony they’ll expel me,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vaggā pana maṃ na nāsessantī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{but if they’re divided they won’t.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idaṃ, ānanda, dutiyaṃ atthavasaṃ sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṅghabhedena nandati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees this as the second reason to relish schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{puna caparaṃ, ānanda, pāpabhikkhu micchāājīvo hoti, micchāājīvena jīvikaṃ kappeti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Furthermore, a bad monk has wrong livelihood and earns a living by wrong livelihood.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa evaṃ hoti:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He thinks:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘sace kho maṃ bhikkhū jānissanti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Suppose the monks know that I have wrong livelihood …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{micchāājīvo micchāājīvena jīvikaṃ kappetīti, samaggā maṃ santā nāsessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{If they’re in harmony they’ll expel me,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vaggā pana maṃ na nāsessantī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{but if they’re divided they won’t.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idaṃ, ānanda, tatiyaṃ atthavasaṃ sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṃghabhedena nandati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees this as the third reason to relish schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{puna caparaṃ, ānanda, pāpabhikkhu lābhakāmo hoti sakkārakāmo anavaññattikāmo.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Furthermore, a bad monk desires material possessions, honor, and admiration.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa evaṃ hoti:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He thinks:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘sace kho maṃ bhikkhū jānissanti—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Suppose the monks know that I desire material possessions, honor, and admiration.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{lābhakāmo sakkārakāmo anavaññattikāmoti, samaggā maṃ santā na sakkarissanti na garuṃ karissanti na mānessanti na pūjessanti;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{If they’re in harmony they won’t honor, respect, revere, or venerate me,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{vaggā pana maṃ sakkarissanti garuṃ karissanti mānessanti pūjessantī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{but if they’re divided they will.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idaṃ, ānanda, catutthaṃ atthavasaṃ sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṅghabhedena nandati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees this as the fourth reason to relish schism in the Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ime kho, ānanda, cattāro atthavase sampassamāno pāpabhikkhu saṅghabhedena nandatī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{A bad monk sees these four reasons to relish schism in the Saṅgha.”}}\\
