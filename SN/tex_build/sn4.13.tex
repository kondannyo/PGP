{\PaliGlossA{13. sakalikasutta}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{13. A Splinter}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evaṃ me sutaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{So I have heard.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā rājagahe viharati maddakucchismiṃ migadāye.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Rājagaha in the Maddakucchi deer park.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tena kho pana samayena bhagavato pādo sakalikāya khato hoti,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Now at that time the Buddha’s foot had been cut by a splinter.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bhusā sudaṃ bhagavato vedanā vattanti sārīrikā dukkhā tibbā kharā kaṭukā asātā amanāpā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The Buddha was stricken by harrowing pains; physical feelings that were painful, sharp, severe, acute, unpleasant, and disagreeable.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tā sudaṃ bhagavā sato sampajāno adhivāseti avihaññamāno.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{But he endured with mindfulness and situational awareness, without worrying.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho bhagavā catugguṇaṃ saṅghāṭiṃ paññapetvā dakkhiṇena passena sīhaseyyaṃ kappesi pāde pādaṃ accādhāya sato sampajāno.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And then he spread out his outer robe folded in four and laid down in the lion’s posture—on the right side, placing one foot on top of the other—mindful and aware.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho māro pāpimā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then Māra the Wicked went up to the Buddha and addressed him in verse:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“mandiyā nu kho sesi udāhu kāveyyamatto,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Are you feeble that you lie down? Or are you drunk on poetry?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atthā nu te sampacurā na santi;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Don’t you have all that you need?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{eko vivitte sayanāsanamhi,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Alone in a secluded lodging,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{niddāmukho kimidaṃ soppase vā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{why this sleeping, sleepyhead?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“na mandiyā sayāmi nāpi kāveyyamatto,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“I’m not feeble that I lie down, nor am I drunk on poetry.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atthaṃ sameccāhamapetasoko;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Having reached the goal, I’m rid of sorrow.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{eko vivitte sayanāsanamhi,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Alone in a secluded lodging,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{sayāmahaṃ sabbabhūtānukampī.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I lie down full of compassion for all living creatures.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yesampi sallaṃ urasi paviṭṭhaṃ,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Even those with a dart stuck in the breast,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{muhuṃ muhuṃ hadayaṃ vedhamānaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{piercing the heart again and again,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tepīdha soppaṃ labhare sasallā,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{are able to get some sleep.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tasmā ahaṃ na supe vītasallo.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{So why not I, whose dart is drawn out?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{jaggaṃ na saṅke napi bhemi sottuṃ,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I don’t lie awake tense, nor do I fear to sleep.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{rattindivā nānutapanti māmaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The days and nights don’t disturb me,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{hāniṃ na passāmi kuhiñci loke,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{as I see no decline for myself in the world.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tasmā supe sabbabhūtānukampī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That’s why I lie down full of compassion for all living creatures.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then Māra the Wicked, thinking, “The Buddha knows me! The Holy One knows me!” miserable and sad, vanished right there.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
