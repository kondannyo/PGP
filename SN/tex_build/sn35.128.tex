{\PaliGlossA{atha kho soṇo gahapatiputto yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. ekamantaṃ nisinno kho soṇo gahapatiputto bhagavantaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
