\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{17. navakammikasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{17. The Builder}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā kosalesu viharati aññatarasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying in the land of the Kosalans in a certain forest grove.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tena kho pana samayena navakammikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo tasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe kammantaṃ kārāpeti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Now at that time the brahmin Bhāradvāja the Builder was doing some building work in that jungle thicket.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{addasā kho navakammikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ aññatarasmiṃ sālarukkhamūle nisinnaṃ pallaṅkaṃ ābhujitvā ujuṃ kāyaṃ paṇidhāya parimukhaṃ satiṃ upaṭṭhapetvā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He saw the Buddha sitting down cross-legged at the root of a certain sal tree, with his body straight, and mindfulness established right there.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{disvānassa etadahosi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Seeing this, it occurred to him,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“ahaṃ kho imasmiṃ vanasaṇḍe kammantaṃ kārāpento ramāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“I enjoy doing this building work here in the jungle.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ayaṃ samaṇo gotamo kiṃ kārāpento ramatī”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I wonder what the ascetic Gotama enjoys doing?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho navakammikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami. upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ gāthāya ajjhabhāsi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then Bhāradvāja the Builder went up to the Buddha and addressed him in verse:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“ke nu kammantā karīyanti,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“What kind of work do you do}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bhikkhu sālavane tava;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{as a mendicant in the sal jungle?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yadekako araññasmiṃ,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{How do you find enjoyment}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ratiṃ vindati gotamo”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{alone in the wilderness, Gotama?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“na me vanasmiṃ karaṇīyamatthi,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“There’s nothing I need to do in the jungle;}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ucchinnamūlaṃ me vanaṃ visūkaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{my jungle’s cut down at the root, it’s withered away.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{svāhaṃ vane nibbanatho visallo,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{With jungle cleared and free of thorns,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{eko rame aratiṃ vippahāyā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I enjoy being alone in the jungle, having given up discontent.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evaṃ vutte, navakammikabhāradvājo brāhmaṇo bhagavantaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When he had spoken, the brahmin Bhāradvāja the Builder said to the Buddha,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“abhikkantaṃ, bho gotama … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Excellent, Master Gotama …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{upāsakaṃ maṃ bhavaṃ gotamo dhāretu ajjatagge pāṇupetaṃ saraṇaṃ gatan”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{From this day forth, may Master Gotama remember me as a lay follower who has gone for refuge for life.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
