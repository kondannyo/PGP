{\PaliGlossA{“yassa kassaci, bhikkhave, bhikkhussa vā bhikkhuniyā vā cakkhuviññeyyesu rūpesu uppajjeyya chando vā rāgo vā doso vā moho vā paṭighaṃ vāpi cetaso, tato cittaṃ nivāreyya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then they’d grab them above the hump and hold them fast there.}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
