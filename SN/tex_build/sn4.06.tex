\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

{\PaliGlossA{atha kho māro pāpimā bhagavato bhayaṃ chambhitattaṃ lomahaṃsaṃ uppādetukāmo mahantaṃ sapparājavaṇṇaṃ abhinimminitvā yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

{\PaliGlossB{Then Māra the Wicked, wanting to make the Buddha feel fear, terror, and goosebumps, manifested in the form of a huge serpent king and approached him.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
