{\PaliGlossB{“Near Benares, in the deer park at Isipatana, the Buddha has rolled forth the supreme Wheel of Dhamma. And that wheel cannot be rolled back by any ascetic or brahmin or god or Māra or Brahmā or by anyone in the world.”}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And so at that moment, in that instant, the cry soared up to the Brahmā realm.}}\\

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
