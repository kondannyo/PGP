{\PaliGlossA{1. moggallānavagga}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}

{\PaliGlossB{1. By Moggallāna}}\\

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{7. ākiñcaññāyatanapañhāsutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{7. A Question About the Dimension of Nothingness}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“‘ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ, ākiñcaññāyatanan’ti vuccati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“They speak of this thing called the ‘dimension of nothingness’.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{katamaṃ nu kho ākiñcaññāyatananti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{What is the dimension of nothingness?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa mayhaṃ, āvuso, etadahosi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{It occurred to me:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘idha bhikkhu sabbaso viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ samatikkamma natthi kiñcīti ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ upasampajja viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘It’s when a mendicant, going totally beyond the dimension of infinite consciousness, aware that “there is nothing at all”, enters and remains in the dimension of nothingness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{idaṃ vuccati ākiñcaññāyatanan’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{This is called the dimension of nothingness.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so khvāhaṃ, āvuso, sabbaso viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ samatikkamma natthi kiñcīti ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ upasampajja viharāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And so … I was entering and remaining in the dimension of nothingness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa mayhaṃ, āvuso, iminā vihārena viharato viññāṇañcāyatanasahagatā saññāmanasikārā samudācaranti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{While I was in that meditation, perceptions and attentions accompanied by the dimension of infinite consciousness beset me.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha kho maṃ, āvuso, bhagavā iddhiyā upasaṅkamitvā etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then the Buddha came up to me with his psychic power and said,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘moggallāna, moggallāna.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Moggallāna, Moggallāna!}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{mā, brāhmaṇa, ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ pamādo, ākiñcaññāyatane cittaṃ saṇṭhapehi, ākiñcaññāyatane cittaṃ ekodiṃ karohi, ākiñcaññāyatane cittaṃ samādahā’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Don’t neglect the dimension of nothingness, brahmin! Settle your mind in the dimension of nothingness; unify your mind and immerse it in the dimension of nothingness.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{so khvāhaṃ, āvuso, aparena samayena sabbaso viññāṇañcāyatanaṃ samatikkamma natthi kiñcīti ākiñcaññāyatanaṃ upasampajja vihāsiṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And so, after some time … I entered and remained in the dimension of nothingness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{yañhi taṃ, āvuso, sammā vadamāno vadeyya … pe … mahābhiññataṃ patto”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{So if anyone should be rightly called a disciple who attained to great direct knowledge with help from the Teacher, it’s me.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{sattamaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{    -}}\\
\end{addmargin}
