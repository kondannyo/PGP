{\PaliGlossA{6. dukkhavagga}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{6. Suffering}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{58. nāmarūpasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{58. Name and Form}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{sāvatthiyaṃ viharati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At Sāvatthī.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“saṃyojaniyesu, bhikkhave, dhammesu assādānupassino viharato nāmarūpassa avakkanti hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“There are things that are prone to being fettered. When you concentrate on the gratification provided by these things, name and form are conceived.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{nāmarūpapaccayā saḷāyatanaṃ … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Name and form are conditions for the six sense fields. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa samudayo hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That is how this entire mass of suffering originates.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, mahārukkho.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose there was a great tree.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{tassa yāni ceva mūlāni adhogamāni, yāni ca tiriyaṅgamāni, sabbāni tāni uddhaṃ ojaṃ abhiharanti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And its roots going downwards and across all draw the sap upwards.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evañhi so, bhikkhave, mahārukkho tadāhāro tadupādāno ciraṃ dīghamaddhānaṃ tiṭṭheyya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Fueled and sustained by that, the great tree would stand for a long time.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evameva kho, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyesu dhammesu assādānupassino viharato nāmarūpassa avakkanti hoti … pe ….}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, there are things that are prone to being fettered. When you concentrate on the gratification provided by these things, name and form are conceived. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{saṃyojaniyesu, bhikkhave, dhammesu ādīnavānupassino viharato nāmarūpassa avakkanti na hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{There are things that are prone to being fettered. When you concentrate on the drawbacks of these things, name and form are not conceived.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{nāmarūpanirodhā saḷāyatananirodho … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When name and form cease, the six sense fields cease. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, mahārukkho.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Suppose there was a great tree.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{atha puriso āgaccheyya kuddālapiṭakaṃ ādāya … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then a person comes along with a spade and basket. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{āyatiṃ anuppādadhammo.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In this way the great tree is cut off at the root, made like a palm stump, obliterated, and unable to arise in the future.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evameva kho, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyesu dhammesu ādīnavānupassino viharato nāmarūpassa avakkanti na hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, there are things that are prone to being fettered. When you concentrate on the drawbacks of these things, name and form are not conceived.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{nāmarūpanirodhā saḷāyatananirodho … pe …}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{When name and form cease, the six sense fields cease. …}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evametassa kevalassa dukkhakkhandhassa nirodho hotī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{That is how this entire mass of suffering ceases.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}
