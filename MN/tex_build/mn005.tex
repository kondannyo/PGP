\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{1. Evaṃ me sutaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{So I have heard.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tatra kho āyasmā sāriputto bhikkhū āmantesi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{There Sāriputta addressed the mendicants:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“āvuso bhikkhave”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Reverends, mendicants!”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Āvuso”ti kho te bhikkhū āyasmato sāriputtassa paccassosuṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Reverend,” they replied.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Āyasmā sāriputto etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Sāriputta said this:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{2. “Cattārome, āvuso, puggalā santo saṃvijjamānā lokasmiṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Mendicants, these four people are found in the world.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Katame cattāro?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{What four?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Idhāvuso, ekacco puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{One person with a blemish doesn’t truly understand: ‘There is a blemish in me.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Idha panāvuso, ekacco puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{But another person with a blemish does truly understand: ‘There is a blemish in me.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Idhāvuso, ekacco puggalo anaṅgaṇova samāno ‘natthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{One person without a blemish doesn’t truly understand: ‘There is no blemish in me.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Idha panāvuso, ekacco puggalo anaṅgaṇova samāno ‘natthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{But another person without a blemish does truly understand: ‘There is no blemish in me.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tatrāvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti, ayaṃ imesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ sāṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ hīnapuriso akkhāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{In this case, of the two persons with a blemish, the one who doesn’t understand is said to be worse,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tatrāvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, ayaṃ imesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ sāṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ seṭṭhapuriso akkhāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{while the one who does understand is better.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tatrāvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo anaṅgaṇova samāno ‘natthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti, ayaṃ imesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ anaṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ hīnapuriso akkhāyati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And of the two persons without a blemish, the one who doesn’t understand is said to be worse,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tatrāvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo anaṅgaṇova samāno ‘natthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ pajānāti, ayaṃ imesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ anaṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ seṭṭhapuriso akkhāyatī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{while the one who does understand is better.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{3. Evaṃ vutte, āyasmā mahāmoggallāno āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{When he said this, Venerable Mahāmoggallāna said to him:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Ko nu kho, āvuso sāriputta, hetu ko paccayo yenimesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ sāṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ eko hīnapuriso akkhāyati, eko seṭṭhapuriso akkhāyati?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“What is the cause, Reverend Sāriputta, what is the reason why, of the two persons with a blemish, one is said to be worse and one better?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Ko panāvuso sāriputta, hetu ko paccayo yenimesaṃ dvinnaṃ puggalānaṃ anaṅgaṇānaṃyeva sataṃ eko hīnapuriso akkhāyati, eko seṭṭhapuriso akkhāyatī”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And what is the cause, what is the reason why, of the two persons without a blemish, one is said to be worse and one better?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{4. “Tatrāvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti, tassetaṃ pāṭikaṅkhaṃ—na chandaṃ janessati na vāyamissati na vīriyaṃ ārabhissati tassaṅgaṇassa pahānāya;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Reverend, take the case of the person who has a blemish and does not understand it. You can expect that they won’t generate enthusiasm, make an effort, or rouse up energy to give up that blemish.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{so sarāgo sadoso samoho sāṅgaṇo saṃkiliṭṭhacitto kālaṃ karissati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And they will die with greed, hate, and delusion, blemished, with a corrupted mind.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Seyyathāpi, āvuso, kaṃsapāti ābhatā āpaṇā vā kammārakulā vā rajena ca malena ca pariyonaddhā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Suppose a bronze dish was brought from a shop or smithy covered with dirt or stains.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tamenaṃ sāmikā na ceva paribhuñjeyyuṃ na ca pariyodapeyyuṃ, rajāpathe ca naṃ nikkhipeyyuṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And the owners neither used it or had it cleaned, but kept it in a dirty place.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Evañhi sā, āvuso, kaṃsapāti aparena samayena saṃkiliṭṭhatarā assa malaggahitā”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Over time, wouldn’t that bronze dish get even dirtier and more stained?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Evamāvuso”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Yes, reverend.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Evameva kho, āvuso, yvāyaṃ puggalo sāṅgaṇova samāno ‘atthi me ajjhattaṃ aṅgaṇan’ti yathābhūtaṃ nappajānāti, tassetaṃ pāṭikaṅkhaṃ—na chandaṃ janessati na vāyamissati na vīriyaṃ ārabhissati tassaṅgaṇassa pahānāya;}}\\

{\PaliColumn{Seyyathāpi, āvuso, kaṃsapāti ābhatā āpaṇā vā kammārakulā vā parisuddhā pariyodātā.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Suppose a bronze dish was brought from a shop or smithy clean and bright.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tamenaṃ sāmikā sālīnaṃ odanaṃ vicitakāḷakaṃ anekasūpaṃ anekabyañjanaṃ racayitvā aññissā kaṃsapātiyā paṭikujjitvā antarāpaṇaṃ paṭipajjeyyuṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Then the owners were to prepare it with boiled fine rice with the dark grains picked out and served with many soups and sauces, cover it with a bronze lid, and parade it through the market-place.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tamenaṃ jano disvā evaṃ vadeyya:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{When people saw it they’d say:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{‘ambho, kimevidaṃ harīyati jaññajaññaṃ viyā’ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{‘My good man, what is it that you’re carrying like a precious treasure?’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tamenaṃ uṭṭhahitvā apāpuritvā olokeyya.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{So they’d open up the lid for people to look inside.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tassa saha dassanena manāpatā ca saṇṭhaheyya, appāṭikulyatā ca saṇṭhaheyya, ajegucchatā ca saṇṭhaheyya;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And as soon as they saw it they were filled with liking, attraction, and relish.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{suhitānampi bhottukamyatā assa, pageva jighacchitānaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Even those who had eaten wanted to eat it, let alone those who were hungry.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Evameva kho, āvuso, yassa kassaci bhikkhuno ime pāpakā akusalā icchāvacarā pahīnā dissanti ceva sūyanti ca, kiñcāpi so hoti gāmantavihārī nemantaniko gahapaticīvaradharo, atha kho naṃ sabrahmacārī sakkaronti garuṃ karonti mānenti pūjenti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{In the same way, when these spheres of bad, unskillful wishes are seen and heard to be given up by a mendicant … their spiritual companions honor, respect, revere, and venerate them.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Taṃ kissa hetu?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Why is that?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Te hi tassa āyasmato pāpakā akusalā icchāvacarā pahīnā dissanti ceva sūyanti cā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{It’s because these spheres of bad, unskillful wishes are seen and heard to be given up by that venerable.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{31. Evaṃ vutte, āyasmā mahāmoggallāno āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{When he said this, Venerable Mahāmoggallāna said to him,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“upamā maṃ, āvuso sāriputta, paṭibhātī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Reverend Sāriputta, a simile springs to mind.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Paṭibhātu taṃ, āvuso moggallānā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Then speak as you feel inspired,” said Sāriputta.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{“Ekamidāhaṃ, āvuso, samayaṃ rājagahe viharāmi giribbaje.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{“Reverend, at one time I was staying right here in Rājagaha, the Mountain Keep.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Atha khvāhaṃ, āvuso, pubbaṇhasamayaṃ nivāsetvā pattacīvaramādāya rājagahaṃ piṇḍāya pāvisiṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Then I robed up in the morning and, taking my bowl and robe, entered Rājagaha for alms.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tena kho pana samayena samīti yānakāraputto rathassa nemiṃ tacchati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Now at that time Samīti the cartwright was planing the rim of a chariot wheel.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Tamenaṃ paṇḍuputto ājīvako purāṇayānakāraputto paccupaṭṭhito hoti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{The <i>Ājīvaka</i> ascetic Paṇḍuputta, who used to be a cartwright, was standing by,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Atha kho, āvuso, paṇḍuputtassa ājīvakassa purāṇayānakāraputtassa evaṃ cetaso parivitakko udapādi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{and this thought came to his mind:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{‘aho vatāyaṃ samīti yānakāraputto imissā nemiyā imañca vaṅkaṃ imañca jimhaṃ imañca dosaṃ taccheyya, evāyaṃ nemi apagatavaṅkā apagatajimhā apagatadosā suddhā assa sāre patiṭṭhitā’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{‘Oh, I hope Samīti the cartwright planes out the crooks, bends, and flaws in this rim. Then the rim will be rid of crooks, bends, and flaws, and consist purely of the essential core.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Yathā yathā kho, āvuso, paṇḍuputtassa ājīvakassa purāṇayānakāraputtassa cetaso parivitakko hoti tathā tathā samīti yānakāraputto tassā nemiyā tañca vaṅkaṃ tañca jimhaṃ tañca dosaṃ tacchati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And Samīti planed out the flaws in the rim just as Paṇḍuputta thought.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Atha kho, āvuso, paṇḍuputto ājīvako purāṇayānakāraputto attamano attamanavācaṃ nicchāresi:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Then Paṇḍuputta expressed his gladness:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{‘hadayā hadayaṃ maññe aññāya tacchatī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{‘He planes like he knows my heart with his heart!’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{32. Evameva kho, āvuso, ye te puggalā assaddhā, jīvikatthā na saddhā agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajitā, saṭhā māyāvino ketabino uddhatā unnaḷā capalā mukharā vikiṇṇavācā, indriyesu aguttadvārā, bhojane amattaññuno, jāgariyaṃ ananuyuttā, sāmaññe anapekkhavanto, sikkhāya na tibbagāravā, bāhulikā sāthalikā, okkamane pubbaṅgamā, paviveke nikkhittadhurā, kusītā hīnavīriyā muṭṭhassatī asampajānā asamāhitā vibbhantacittā duppaññā eḷamūgā, tesaṃ āyasmā sāriputto iminā dhammapariyāyena hadayā hadayaṃ maññe aññāya tacchati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{In the same way, there are those faithless people who went forth from the lay life to homelessness not out of faith but to earn a livelihood. They’re devious, deceitful, and sneaky. They’re restless, insolent, fickle, gossipy, and loose-tongued. They do not guard their sense doors or eat in moderation, and they are not dedicated to wakefulness. They don’t care about the ascetic life, and don’t keenly respect the training. They’re indulgent and slack, leaders in backsliding, neglecting seclusion, lazy, and lacking energy. They’re unmindful, lacking situational awareness and immersion, with straying minds, witless and stupid. Venerable Sāriputta planes their faults with this exposition of the teaching as if he knows my heart with his heart!}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Ye pana te kulaputtā saddhā agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajitā, asaṭhā amāyāvino aketabino anuddhatā anunnaḷā acapalā amukharā avikiṇṇavācā, indriyesu guttadvārā, bhojane mattaññuno, jāgariyaṃ anuyuttā, sāmaññe apekkhavanto, sikkhāya tibbagāravā, na bāhulikā na sāthalikā, okkamane nikkhittadhurā, paviveke pubbaṅgamā, āraddhavīriyā pahitattā upaṭṭhitassatī sampajānā samāhitā ekaggacittā paññavanto aneḷamūgā, te āyasmato sāriputtassa imaṃ dhammapariyāyaṃ sutvā pivanti maññe, ghasanti maññe vacasā ceva manasā ca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{But there are those gentlemen who went forth from the lay life to homelessness out of faith. They’re not devious, deceitful, and sneaky. They’re not restless, insolent, fickle, gossipy, and loose-tongued. They guard their sense doors and eat in moderation, and they are dedicated to wakefulness. They care about the ascetic life, and keenly respect the training. They’re not indulgent or slack, nor are they leaders in backsliding, neglecting seclusion. They’re energetic and determined. They’re mindful, with situational awareness, immersion, and unified minds; wise, not stupid. Hearing this exposition of the teaching from Venerable Sāriputta, they drink it up and devour it, as it were. And in speech and thought they say:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{‘sādhu vata, bho, sabrahmacārī akusalā vuṭṭhāpetvā kusale patiṭṭhāpetī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{‘It’s good, sirs, that he draws his spiritual companions away from the unskillful and establishes them in the skillful.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{33. Seyyathāpi, āvuso, itthī vā puriso vā daharo yuvā maṇḍanakajātiko sīsaṃnhāto uppalamālaṃ vā vassikamālaṃ vā atimuttakamālaṃ vā labhitvā ubhohi hatthehi paṭiggahetvā uttamaṅge sirasmiṃ patiṭṭhapeyya;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{Suppose there was a woman or man who was young, youthful, and fond of adornments, and had bathed their head. After getting a garland of lotuses, jasmine, or liana flowers, they would take them in both hands and place them on the crown of the head.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{evameva kho, āvuso, ye te kulaputtā saddhā agārasmā anagāriyaṃ pabbajitā, asaṭhā amāyāvino aketabino anuddhatā anunnaḷā acapalā amukharā avikiṇṇavācā, indriyesu guttadvārā, bhojane mattaññuno, jāgariyaṃ anuyuttā, sāmaññe apekkhavanto, sikkhāya tibbagāravā, na bāhulikā na sāthalikā, okkamane nikkhittadhurā, paviveke pubbaṅgamā, āraddhavīriyā pahitattā upaṭṭhitassatī sampajānā samāhitā ekaggacittā paññavanto aneḷamūgā, te āyasmato sāriputtassa imaṃ dhammapariyāyaṃ sutvā pivanti maññe, ghasanti maññe vacasā ceva manasā ca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{In the same way, those gentlemen who went forth from the lay life to homelessness out of faith … say:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{‘sādhu vata, bho, sabrahmacārī akusalā vuṭṭhāpetvā kusale patiṭṭhāpetī’”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{‘It’s good, sirs, that he draws his spiritual companions away from the unskillful and establishes them in the skillful.’”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliColumn{Itiha te ubho mahānāgā aññamaññassa subhāsitaṃ samanumodiṃsūti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\EnglishColumn{And so these two spiritual giants agreed with each others’ fine words.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

{\PaliColumn{Anaṅgaṇasuttaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ pañcamaṃ.}}\\
