\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s monastery.}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{2. Tena kho pana samayena jāṇussoṇi brāhmaṇo sabbasetena vaḷavābhirathena sāvatthiyā niyyāti divādivassa.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Now at that time the brahmin Jāṇussoṇi drove out from Sāvatthī in the middle of the day in an all-white chariot drawn by mares.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Addasā kho jāṇussoṇi brāhmaṇo pilotikaṃ paribbājakaṃ dūratova āgacchantaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He saw the wanderer Pilotika coming off in the distance,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Disvāna pilotikaṃ paribbājakaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{and said to him,}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Handa kuto nu bhavaṃ vacchāyano āgacchati divādivassā”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“So, Master Vacchāyana, where are you coming from in the middle of the day?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Ito hi kho ahaṃ, bho, āgacchāmi samaṇassa gotamassa santikā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}

\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“What do you think of the ascetic Gotama’s lucidity of wisdom? Do you think he’s astute?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Ko cāhaṃ, bho, ko ca samaṇassa gotamassa paññāveyyattiyaṃ jānissāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“My good man, who am I to judge the ascetic Gotama’s lucidity of wisdom?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Sopi nūnassa tādisova yo samaṇassa gotamassa paññāveyyattiyaṃ jāneyyā”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{You’d really have to be on the same level to judge his lucidity of wisdom.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Uḷārāya khalu bhavaṃ vacchāyano samaṇaṃ gotamaṃ pasaṃsāya pasaṃsatī”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Master Vacchāyana praises the ascetic Gotama with lofty praise indeed.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Ko cāhaṃ, bho, ko ca samaṇaṃ gotamaṃ pasaṃsissāmi,}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Who am I to praise the ascetic Gotama?}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{pasatthapasatthova so bhavaṃ gotamo seṭṭho devamanussānan”ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{He is praised by the praised as the best of gods and humans.”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“But for what reason are you so devoted to the ascetic Gotama?”}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘The Blessed One is a fully awakened Buddha. The teaching is well explained. The Saṅgha is practicing well.’}}\\

\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
