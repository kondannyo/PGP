\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Cūḷasuññatasutta}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{The Shorter Discourse on Emptiness}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Evaṃ me sutaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{So I have heard.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati pubbārāme migāramātupāsāde.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{At one time the Buddha was staying near Sāvatthī in the Eastern Monastery, the stilt longhouse of Migāra’s mother.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Atha kho āyasmā ānando sāyanhasamayaṃ paṭisallānā vuṭṭhito yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā ānando bhagavantaṃ etadavoca:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Then in the late afternoon, Venerable Ānanda came out of retreat and went to the Buddha. He bowed, sat down to one side, and said to him:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Ekamidaṃ, bhante, samayaṃ bhagavā sakkesu viharati nagarakaṃ nāma sakyānaṃ nigamo.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Sir, this one time the Buddha was staying in the land of the Sakyans where they have a town named Nagaraka.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Tattha me, bhante, bhagavato sammukhā sutaṃ, sammukhā paṭiggahitaṃ:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{There I heard and learned this in the presence of the Buddha:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘suññatāvihārenāhaṃ, ānanda, etarahi bahulaṃ viharāmī’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Ānanda, these days I usually practice the meditation on emptiness.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Kacci metaṃ, bhante, sussutaṃ suggahitaṃ sumanasikataṃ sūpadhāritan”ti?}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{I trust I properly heard, learned, attended, and remembered that from the Buddha?”}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{“Taggha te etaṃ, ānanda, sussutaṃ suggahitaṃ sumanasikataṃ sūpadhāritaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{“Indeed, Ānanda, you properly heard, learned, attended, and remembered that.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Pubbepāhaṃ, ānanda, etarahipi suññatāvihārena bahulaṃ viharāmi.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Now, as before, I usually practice the meditation on emptiness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\vskip 0.05in
\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Seyyathāpi, ānanda, ayaṃ migāramātupāsādo suñño hatthigavassavaḷavena, suñño jātarūparajatena, suñño itthipurisasannipātena atthi cevidaṃ asuññataṃ yadidaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Consider this stilt longhouse of Migāra’s mother. It’s empty of elephants, cows, horses, and mares; of gold and money; and of gatherings of men and women.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{bhikkhusaṃghaṃ paṭicca ekattaṃ;}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{There is only this that is not emptiness, namely, the oneness dependent on the mendicant Saṅgha.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{evameva kho, ānanda, bhikkhu amanasikaritvā gāmasaññaṃ, amanasikaritvā manussasaññaṃ, araññasaññaṃ paṭicca manasi karoti ekattaṃ.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{In the same way, a mendicant—ignoring the perception of the village and the perception of people—focuses on the oneness dependent on the perception of wilderness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Tassa araññasaññāya cittaṃ pakkhandati pasīdati santiṭṭhati adhimuccati.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{Their mind becomes eager, confident, settled, and decided in that perception of wilderness.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{So evaṃ pajānāti:}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They understand:}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{‘ye assu darathā gāmasaññaṃ paṭicca tedha na santi, ye assu darathā manussasaññaṃ paṭicca tedha na santi, atthi cevāyaṃ darathamattā yadidaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{‘Here there is no stress due to the perception of village or the perception of people.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{araññasaññaṃ paṭicca ekattan’ti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{There is only this modicum of stress, namely the oneness dependent on the perception of wilderness.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{So ‘suññamidaṃ saññāgataṃ gāmasaññāyā’ti pajānāti, ‘suññamidaṃ saññāgataṃ manussasaññāyā’ti pajānāti, ‘atthi cevidaṃ asuññataṃ yadidaṃ—}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{They understand: ‘This field of perception is empty of the perception of the village. It is empty of the perception of people.}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{araññasaññaṃ paṭicca ekattan’ti.}}\\

\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{There is only this that is not emptiness, namely the oneness dependent on the perception of wilderness.’}}\\
\end{addmargin}
\end{absolutelynopagebreak}

\begin{absolutelynopagebreak}
\setstretch{.7}
{\PaliGlossA{Iti yañhi kho tattha na hoti tena taṃ suññaṃ samanupassati, yaṃ pana tattha avasiṭṭhaṃ hoti taṃ ‘santamidaṃ atthī’ti pajānāti.}}\\
\begin{addmargin}[1em]{2em}
\setstretch{.5}
{\PaliGlossB{And so they regard it as empty of what is not there, but as to what remains they understand that it is present.}}\\
